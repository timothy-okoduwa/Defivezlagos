THE LOBBY BAR




THE SPACE
We offer the Lobby Bar to small groups who wish to use the space for work and personal events. Our team will be there to ensure everything goes according to plan. The Lobby Bar offers an intimate setting for up to 20 people and food and beverages can be ordered from neighboring Circa Lagos



PARTNERSHIPS
We also work with companies who have periodic team-building activities and offer a discounted rate for hotel rooms if the exercise takes place over a few days neighboring Circa Lagos
With its comfy sofas and armchairs, The Lobby Bar is a cozy space for friends and colleagues to meet recreationally or professionally to enjoy light meals, snacks and cocktails throughout the day and evening.


Located in the Borough Lagos Hotel, and right beside the popular Circa Lagos Restaurant, the bar is in the heart of Lagos. Just 5km away from Victoria Island, the business hub of Lagos and in close proximity to main attractions like the Nike Art Gallery, Palms shopping mall and spectacular nightlife. The thoughtfully decorated space is a showcase of unique design touches, such as the vintage typewriters adorning the walls and the carefully curated artisanal liquors lined up in the glowing bar.







For any general enquiries regarding Booking the Lobby Bar for events and gatherings, please call us on 0704 712 4999 or email bookings@defivezlagos.com and we will endeavour to respond within 24 hours (Monday to Friday).
