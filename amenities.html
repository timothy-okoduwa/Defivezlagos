Our Amenities




The Boardroom



Our purpose built meeting room can sit a maximum of 8 people and is perfect for private meetings. The space features modern audiovisual equipment and is available for a day rental or a morning or afternoon rental.
Garden Terrace



Our private Garden Terrace is located on level 1 and is exclusive to in-house guests. This terrace is perfect for guests to relax and enjoy the outdoors over a drink or two.
Villa 24



Villa 24 is our stylish guest lounge exclusive to in-house guests and located on the ground level. The lounge offers plush sofas and soft background music, an ideal space for our guests to relax and rejuvenate.
Fitness Villa



Our well-equipped Fitness Villa is located next to the lobby. The fitness villa features a range of cardiovascular and weight machines purposed to meet your health and fitness goals.
