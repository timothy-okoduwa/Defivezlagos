The Offa room at De Fivez is a blast of vibrant African energy and creative beauty. The cultural mix of colors combines to give a native yet relaxing vibe. Walk into a world of native culture and arts all within one room. The classic earthy and vibrant colors of African décor style in this room begin with the beige wall and carry through to the various tones of high-spirited Ankara prints, earthy brown, grey, and white. The black and white mix with the traditional prints creates a classy yet festive mix of nature and culture. Offa room features a Queen-sized upholstered bed in black-brown and bright Ankara headboards. African-themed artworks work alongside all the other hallmarks of African décor style; natural fibers, wood, and linen. Overall, this room is all about luxury and harmony with nature—the textured gray accent wall with wood paneling. Open windows separate the beige walls for the perfect view, each laced with high-grade, color-plain mix curtains.
